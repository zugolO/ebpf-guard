## How to add your entry

Open a pull request that adds a row to the appropriate table:

```markdown
| Your Org | Brief description of use-case | ebpf-guard version | Environment |
```

Fields:
- **Org** — company name, team name, or "individual" (no contact details required).
- **Use-case** — one sentence: what you are detecting or enforcing.
- **Version** — the earliest version you evaluated (`git describe --tags`).
- **Environment** — e.g. "Kubernetes 1.31 on AWS EKS", "bare-metal Ubuntu 24.04".

You can remain anonymous: `| Anonymous financial institution | ... |`.
